# Personas and Roles

## Roles (v0)

### Owner
**Responsibilities**
- Defines pricing, recipes, and operating standards
- Reviews event profitability and replenishment needs

**Permissions**
- Full access to operational docs and system notes
- Can define or modify roles, workflow standards, and inventory rules

### Event Staff
**Responsibilities**
- Runs the booth during event day
- Logs batches and sales counts as required by workflow

**Permissions**
- Can follow workflows and record event-day counts
- Does not change pricing, cost assumptions, or recipe definitions
