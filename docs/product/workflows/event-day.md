# Event Day Workflow

## Goal
Operate the booth with minimal friction while capturing enough information to reconcile the day and improve restocking decisions.

## Before opening (15 minutes)
- [ ] Confirm booth setup complete
- [ ] Confirm starting inventory counts (high-level)
- [ ] Confirm price list and payment methods available
- [ ] Confirm labeling for flavors and allergens (if applicable)

## During service
### Production (batches)
For each batch:
- [ ] Record **flavor**
- [ ] Record **time started**
- [ ] Record **sellable units produced** (e.g., bags)
- [ ] Record **waste** (if any) and reason

### Sales
At defined intervals (e.g., hourly) or when practical:
- [ ] Record units sold by flavor (approximate is acceptable if consistent)
- [ ] Record payment split (cash/card/other) if tracked
- [ ] Note anomalies (refunds, freebies, major waste)

## Close down (20–30 minutes)
- [ ] Record ending inventory counts for key ingredients and packaging
- [ ] Record remaining sellable units by flavor
- [ ] Secure cash and record total cash-on-hand
- [ ] Capture any operational notes (weather, traffic, issues, wins)

## Outputs
- Batch log for the event
- Sales summary (by flavor)
- Notes for reconciliation and future planning
